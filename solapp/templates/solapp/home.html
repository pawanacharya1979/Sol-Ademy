Established in 2007 in Nayabazar, SEC (Sol Eduacation Consultancy Ptv Ltd.) walked with the aim of facilitating the student who wants to go to JAPAN for their further study and make their career out of it. Currently, SEC is located in Dillibazar, Kathmandu, Nepal. And Today, every students positively share about SEC story to their friends, colleagues and family members. And this is what SEC has earned in past 10 years.

SEC is here not only for providing the counselling and providing the support for getting VISA to the students, rather SEC is also known for providing friendly behaviors, making feel them SEC like their home, taking care physically and mentally too. SEC don't stop its caring behavior, even after the students are boarded to JAPAN and frequently keep following thier traces on what they are doing, are they having any difficulties in living and fooding in JAPAN, or in Schools or in Language and so on. So, SEC is very much concerned on their physical, mental and financial status in JAPAN. Hence, students has also placed us in one of the respectable status as a "Family" in their life.

SEC believes that going to JAPAN only is not a big thing, but after going to JAPAN, sociolizing with the their culture, and being as a part of JAPANESE environment is what matters a lot. So, before going to JAPAN, students must learn about JAPAN, how to cope with the JAPANESE environment, and must be fluent with JAPANESE Language. And this is the Key Success factor for making a way to JAPAN.

At Last, i am very thankful to my SEC Team members for supporting me in each and every moment, our loving students, good wishers, Japanese Schools and all the other stakeholders. Thank You...
