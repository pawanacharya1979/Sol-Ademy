Student Visa

SEC provide application process of student Visa. It is sad
to say that many of Nepali people have already entered
Japan and been staying there.

Qualification

Academic background:--> above +2 / intermediate
Age:--> below 26 years old

Required Documents

Documents to certificate applicant

  • All of academic certificates with transcripts
  • Photocopy of original citizenship certificate and English translation of that
  • Passport photocopy if you have issued passport
  • Essay (purpose of study in Japan)
  • Recommendation letter from campus or working place which the student belongs
  • Gap certificate if the student has some gap in academic history
  • Other document may be required

Documents to certificate sponsor

  • Bank Balance certificate with statement for 3 years
  • Annual income certificate for 3 years
  • Occupation letter if your sponsor is employee
  • Company registration certificate if your sponsor runs company by himself
  • Other supported documents to prove how income generated
  • Property valuation
  • Tax clearance for 3 years
  • Relationship certificate to make sure the relation between the student and his sponsor
  • Sponsor letter

Please visit SEC for counseling with detail information.
