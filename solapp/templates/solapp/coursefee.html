Courses & Fee Structure

Although there are 51 administrative districts in Japan, everyone only try to go to big city like Tokyo, Osaka and so on. But other cities are not so much different from those big cities. In remote area, job opportunities are a little bit less or salary is a little less than those big cities, but living expenses like room rent are also less in remote areas. So SEC think wherever students go, the living condition is same. According to this policy, SEC made wide connections all over Japan. You can choose most suitable one from our various options. At first when you entered Japan, you have to learn Japanese language for 1, 2 year(s). It seems that to learn Japanese language again in Japan is wasting money and time, but fluent Japanese language skill is necessary to stay in Japan efficiently. There for SEC recommend this process.
